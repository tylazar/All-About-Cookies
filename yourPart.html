Your Part

How cookies affect the average user - as we've seen, cookies are commonly used for personalized advertisements - they also allow you to use the modern Web though, e.g. saving settings or logins Privacy concerns with cookies - users have concerns about privacy on the web, namely: - anonymity - unintended use of cookies - surreptitious nature of data collection - trespassing into the web users' own resources - threat of cookie sharing (online privacy concerns) Steps to take to protect your privacy - delete cookies regularly - use ad blockers - set up "do not track" requests in your browser - don't accept terms if you don't understand them
